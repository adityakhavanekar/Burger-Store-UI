ABOUT US

AK's Burger Point Corporation is an Indian fast food company, founded in 2015 as a restaurant operated by Aditya Khavanekar , in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. We rechristened our business as a hamburger stand, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the Golden Arches logo being introduced in 2016 at a location in Thane, Maharashtra. In 2017, a businessman joined the company as a franchise agent and proceeded to purchase the chain from the AK's Burger Point owners.

AK's Burger Point had its previous headquarters in Chembur, Maharashtra, but moved its global headquarters to Thane in June 2018.
